1. Korewa anatano no-todes ka?
- Hai, korwa watashino no-todes.
2. Korewa kanojono kagides ka?
- Iie, korwa anatano kagides.
3. Anatano namaewa nanides ka?
- Watashino namaewa Konraddes.
4. Ohayou gozaimas, korewa watashino bi-rudes ka?
- Ohayou gozaimas, iie, korewa watashino bi-rudes.
5. Korewa nanides ka?
- Korwa pendes.
6. Kareno namaewa Tomekdes ka?
- Hai, kareno namaewa Tomekdes.
7. Konbanwa, watashino namaewa Konraddes.
