## New grammar

1. `no` - glue between adjectives and nouns
2. `nan` - missing value; "what"

### Examples

| Japanese                   | English            |
| -------------------------- | ------------------ |
| Watasino pendes            | My pen             |
| Anatano pendes             | Your pen           |
| Anatano namaewa nandes ka? | What is your name? |
