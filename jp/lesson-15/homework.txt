translate & then answer in Japanese:
1. Your younger sister’s house is in Belgium? (IIE,...)*
2. Today your friends go to a theater?
3. Your friend’s older sister is in the shop next to the house? (IIE,...)
4. You will go to a shop now? (IIE,...)
5. Your name is Pafnucy? (IIE,...)
6. Rabbits are in the botanical garden in this city?
7. Chickens are in the garden? (IIE,...)
8. This car is going to which city?
9. “Where are you going today?”
10. Elephants are in the zoo in Wrocław?

*(IIE,...) = please start the answer with "IIE" & use negation.
