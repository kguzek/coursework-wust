1. Kono densha wa Warushaua ni ikimas. Sono densha mo Warushaua ni ikimas ka?
Hai, sono densha mo Warushaua ni ikimas.
2. Kyou anata wa Nihongo no benkyou o shimas. Ashita mo anata wa nihongo no benkyou o shimas ka?
Iie, ashita mo watashi wa nihongo no benkyou wa shimasen.
3. Anata no tomodachi wa ryouri o shimas. Kare wa anata no shukudai mo shimas ka?
Iie, kare wa watashi no shukudai wa shimasen.
4. Watashi wa kono basu de ie ni kaerimas. Anata mo kono basu de ie ni kaerimas ka?
Hai, watashi mo kono basu de ie ni kaerimas.
5. Ashita anata wa mise ni ikimas. Anata wa yuubinkyoku ni mo ikimas ka?
Hai, watashi wa yuubinkyoku ni mo ikimas.
6. Kyou watashi wa tomodachi to basuketto bo-ru o shimas. Anata mo watashitachi to yakyuu o shimas ka?
Hai, watashi mo anatatachi to yakyuu o shimas.
7. Dare ga anata to kaimono o shimas ka?
Tomodachi ga watashi to kaimono o shimas.
8. Kyou dare ga resutoran de hitori de shokuji o shimas ka?
Kyou chichi ga resutoran de hitori de shokuji o shimas.
9. Dare ga watashi no shukudai o shimas ka?
Anata ga anata no shukudai o shimas.
