1. Kono densha wa Warushaua ni ikimas. Sono densha mo Warushaua ni ikimas ka?
Hai, sono densha mo Warushaua ni ikimas.
2. Kyou anata wa Nihongo no benkyou o shimas. Ashita anata mo nihongo no benkyou o shimas ka?
Iie, ashita watashi wa nihongo no benkyou wa shimasen.
3. Anata no tomodachi wa ryouri o shimas. Kare mo anata no shukudai o shimas ka?
Iie, kare wa watashi no shukudai wa shimasen.
4. Watashi wa kono basu de ie ni kaerimas. Anata mo kono basu de ie ni kaerimas ka?
Hai, watashi mo kono basu de ie ni kaerimas.
5. Ashita anata wa mise ni ikimas. Anata mo yuubinkyoku ni ikimas ka?
Hai, watashi mo yuubinkyoku ni ikimas.
6. Kyou watashi wa tomodachi to basuketto bo-ru o shimas. Anata mo watashitachi to basuketto bo-ru o shimas ka?
Hai, watashi mo anatatachi to basuetto bo-ru o shimas.
7. Dare ga anata to kaimono o shimas ka?
Tomodachi wa watashi to kaimono o shimas.
8. Kyou dare ga resutoran de hitori de shokuji o shimas ka?
Kyou chichi wa resutoran de hitori de shokuji o shimas.
9. Dare ga watashi no shukudai o shimas ka?
Anata wa anata no shukudai o shimas.
