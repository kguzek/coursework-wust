| ---------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------ |
| Anata wa dochira no machi no kata des ka?            | Which city are you from?       |
| Watashi wa Wrocław no hito des.                      | I am from Wrocław.             |
| Watashi wa Chrząszczyżewoszyce no machi no hito des. | I am from Chrząszczyżewoszyce. |
