Homework A1 60b
Last update: 07.01.2026

translate & answer the questions in Japanese:
1. You drive what?
2. You clean what this morning?
3. Your friends play football? or baseball?
4. This evening you will do what, using a vacuum cleaner?
5. You play what game this evening?
6. You study what tomorrow?
7. You do what homework?
8. Today you study Japanese? or English?
9. Your older brother wash what with this washing machine?
10. You take a dog for a walk today?

vocabulary:
unten = driving (a motorized vehicle with wheels)
***
language = [country/language name] + go
for example,
Japanese language = nihongo
Polish language = po-randogo
***
English language = eigo
***
football = sakka-
volleyball = bare-bo-ru
baseball = yakyuu
basketball = basuketto bo-ru
swimming = suiei
