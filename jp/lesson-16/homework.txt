Last update: 08.12.2025

translate and then answer the questions:
1. Your father is going to a forest? or to a river?
2. Tomorrow morning your family goes to Japan? 
3. When you go home?
4. Your younger brother goes to a lake today? or tomorrow?
5. When you go to cinema?
6. When your mother is going to a mountain? 
7. This evening* you will be at home? or go to university?
8. This afternoon your friend goes home? or stays** at the police station?

answer the questions in Japanese:
9. kyou no itsu anata no ani wa ie ni imaska?
10. itsu no yuugata anata wa doubutsuen ni ikimaska?

* this evening = today's evening ("this" means "today's").  The same for "this morning" and "this afternoon" 
** use "iru" or "aru" to translate "to stay"

vocabulary:
morning = asa
afternoon = hiru
late afternoon (16:00-19:00) = yuugata
evening/night = yoru

vocabulary for the next lesson: 
this morning = kesa (short form of “kyou no asa”)
this evening = kon ya (short form of “kyou no yoru”)
