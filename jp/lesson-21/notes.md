## New vocabulary

| Japanese | English |
| -------- | ------- |
| To       | With    |
| Hitoride | Alone   |

### New vocabulary – examples

| Japanese                                        | English                               |
| ----------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------- |
| Watashi wa benkyou o ie de tomodachi to shimas. | I am studying at home with my friend. |
| Anata wa benkyou o dare to shimas ka?           | With whom are you studying?           |
| Watashi wa benkyou o hitori de shimas.          | I am studying alone.                  |

## New grammar

1. For level 4 constructions (suru), we use `de` as the location connector.
2. The `de` connector can be used to indicate the nature of a group of people doing an activity together\*!.
3. The `to` connector is used to mean _with_ or _accompaniment_.

### New grammar – examples

| Japanese                               | English                                         |
| -------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------- |
| Watashi wa benkyou o ie de shimas.     | I am studying at home.                          |
| Doko de anata wa benkyou o shimas ka?  | Where are you going to study?                   |
| Karera wa pabu ni tomodachi de ikimas. | They (a group of friends) are going to the pub. |
