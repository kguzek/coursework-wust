## New grammar

1. The `ga` connector is also used if any part of the noun phrase is unknown. This includes all question placeholders:
   - dare
   - yoko
   - nani
   - itsu
   - dore
   - dochira
2. When answering "which" questions which have no answer, we use `mo` with a negative verb.
