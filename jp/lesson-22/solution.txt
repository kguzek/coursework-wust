1. Watashi wa mise to daigaku ni kyou ikimas.
2. Watashi wa tomodachi to sakka- to benkyou o shimas.
3. Kyou anata wa souji to shukudai o shimas.
4. Ashi to basu de watashi wa mura ni ikimas.
5. Watashi wa Ania to Tomek to benkyou o shimas.
6. Anata wa kon ya chichi to haha to ie de shokuji o shimas ka?
Hai, watashi wa kon ya chichi to haha to ie de shokuji o shimas.
7. Anata no imouto wa eigo to furansugo no benkyou o daigaku de shimas ka?
Iie, watashi no imouto wa nihongo no benkyou o daigaku de shimas.
8. Anata wa watashi no inu to anata no inu no sanpo o shimas ka?
Hai, watashi wa anata no inu to watashi no inu no sanpo o shimas.
9. Kyou to ashita, anata to anata no tomodachi wa doko ni imas ka?
Kyou to ashita, watashi to watashi no tomodachi wa pabu ni imas.
10. Watashi wa nihongo to shi pra pra no shukudai o kyou shimas.
11. Watashi no ane to imouto wa korekara pabu to daigaku ni kuruma de ikimas.
12. Watashi wa ie to kuruma no souji o ie de shimas.
13. Watashi wa tomodachi to itoko to fuku to kutsu to boushi no sentaku o shimas.
